What did I stumble across?

This site was created with the sole purpose of explaining and exploring horror series / ARGs around the internet. For all fans of unfiction, horror, and general enjoyers fear-inducing things around the internet, this website is for you.

The goals of this wiki are as follows:

  • Catalog horror ARGs and stories from around the internet.
  • Provide a centralized place to find new series.
  • Give a place for discussion about the meaning behind the aformentioned series.

If you enjoy consuming this type of content, or are looking to discover more about this neesh internet genre, this website is for you.


What exactly does this site cover?

This site covers all things horror on the internet, from ARGs to independent series. There are several genres of sorts within this side of the internet, and this site covers all of them. Analog horror, found footage, and even art projects are read and analyzed on this site for the enjoyment of the genre.
